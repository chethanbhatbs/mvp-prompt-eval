What it means

  • The split works. Free users reach a preview within 10 credits far more often — Normal 53% → v2 77% / eph3 70%.
  • Win is on long prompts. P1: Normal never previews in budget (10–11 ECU); both split arms hit it at ~6 ECU (100%).
  • eph3 is the pick. Highest quality (7.7) and previews early within the cap, so users see value fast — without under-building. (v2 is cheapest on total ECU; eph3 ≈ Normal on total cost — its edge is quality + early preview, not raw cost.)
  • v2 is cheapest but thin. On the complex Task app its best build scored 4/10 — it cuts scope to hit the cap.
  • P3 is unsolved. Every arm previews only at 12–22 ECU — over the cap regardless; needs a separate fix.
  • Reliable & fair. 2,637 LLM calls · 0.3% errors (all timeout-retries) · 0 compaction · spread evenly across arms.
Decision: ship MVP-split → adopt eph3. Separate workstream for P3-class heavy prompts. Live functional E2E re-run pending preview-pod revival (6/6 passed where live).
